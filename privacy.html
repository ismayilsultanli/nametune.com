Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

NameTune ("we", "our", or "us") operates the NameTune mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in via Google, Facebook, or Microsoft, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from the provider. We do not receive or store your password.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics about app usage, such as which features are used and crash reports, to improve the app experience.

Content You Create: Song lyrics, audio files, and related metadata that you generate using the app are stored to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the NameTune service
  • To personalize your experience and generated content
  • To process transactions and manage your credits
  • To send notifications about your audio generation status
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns
  • To detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized use

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted in the EU, Ireland region) with row-level security policies. Audio files are stored on secure cloud servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL).

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database (EU region)
  • Google Sign-In, Facebook Login, Microsoft Login — Authentication providers
  • Sentry — Error tracking and crash reporting
  • AI Services — For generating lyrics and audio content (no personal data is sent to AI models beyond the creative input you provide)

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data and created content for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@nametune.com.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@nametune.com.

7. Children's Privacy

NameTune is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The children's audio category (lullabies, stories) is intended for parents and guardians to create content for their children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@nametune.com

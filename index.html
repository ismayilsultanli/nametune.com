About Us

Music made personal

NameTune was born from a simple idea: every person deserves a song that's truly theirs. We combine advanced AI technology with deep musical understanding to create personalized audio experiences — from lullabies with your baby's name to birthday anthems for your best friend.

Our mission is to make the joy of personalized music accessible to everyone, in every language, for every occasion. Whether it's a graduation gift, a motivational track, or a bedtime story, NameTune turns your words into unforgettable audio moments.
